Instructions

Welcome to the pilot PISA 2030 reading test. Please read the introduction.

Technical introduction:

This is an interactive reading test. You will be answering questions in a simulated chat based on an algorithm, meaning that you are not conversing with a human.
Note that once you have sent an answer in the chat, you cannot return or change that answer.
On the left of the screen, a chat box is present.

  • Your friend's messages are shown in white.
  • Instructions are shown in green.
  • Your answers will be shown in blue.

On the right of the screen, a selection of texts is shown.

Scenario introduction:

  • In this reading test, you will be responding to your friend [Jamie/Chad McChatty] who has recently visited Rapa Nui. They want to share and discuss their beliefs about the island with you via this online chat.
  • You will collect 4 texts with information about Rapa Nui that you will use during the discussion with [Jamie/Chad McChatty]. Read the texts and answer your friend's questions.

When you are ready, click on [START].
